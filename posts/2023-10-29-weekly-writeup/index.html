weekly-writeup (week 4 of Oct 2023)

Posted on Oct 29, 2023

Highlights

  • had a bee try and take me out while picking up the kids
  • lots of wins doing managerial tasks
  • ended up watching a really great video by Amy Hupe
  • got a few runs in!

Takeaways

Had a nice week that was largerly drawn out with meetings at work and a lots of journaling at home. Wasnt able to meet the daily meditations sessions I wanted, but did get in more running which was great. I’m still reading the book ‘Build’ from Tony Fadell and its almost impossible to read because I want to write notes throughout. The ideas parallel so many of the things ive run into during my stretch as a CTO and really calls to question just what you should be doing for a customer journey for your application and product. Its a really great book and can’t wait to do a larger writeup.

Other than that, its hard to fit a week into a few paragraphs, but the exercise is a fun exercise.
