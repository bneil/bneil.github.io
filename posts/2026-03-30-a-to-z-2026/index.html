The A to Z Challenge

I signed up for the April Blogging from A to Z Challenge! The challenge is simple: blog every day in April, with each day corresponding to a letter of the alphabet.

The theme this year is “Aspirations: Blogging hopes, dreams, and goals”

The Complete A-to-Z Collection

Here’s every post I’ll write as part of this challenge. Enjoy the short or long exploration into my blogging aspirations.

  • 2026-04-01-a-for-aspiration - Letter A, Whats your aspiration
  • 2026-04-02-brainstorming - Letter B, What am I writing about
  • 2026-04-03-creativity - Letter C, Getting creative
  • 2026-04-04-dream - Letter D, to dream
  • 2026-04-06-events - Letter E, Events
  • 2026-04-07-frames - Letter F, Frames
  • 2026-04-08-ghost - Letter G, Ghost
  • 2026-04-09-hobby - Letter H, Hobby
  • 2026-04-10-interesting - Letter I, Interesting
  • 2026-04-11-justfile - Letter J, justfile
  • 2026-04-13-lazy - Letter L, Lazy
  • 2026-04-14-multitasking - Letter M, Multitasking
## Why I'm Doing This

After completing the 50of50 challenge, I wanted to try another blogging challenge. So I did a quick kagi search and the A-to-Z challenge came up. I love a good reason to write, so im excited to get after it.

Check out the official A-to-Z Challenge site! And join up if its not to late

wish me luck
