## E is for Events

I wanted to write a small bit about Events, mainly the ones we choose to celebrate and ones we choose ignore.
This is a little personal, so if you want to skip to another article, id go to that now.

My old man hates his birthday. Or at least ive never known him to enjoy it. Same with Christmas and same with most holidays.

Ive seen him stand it, seen him sit through it but I never saw him choosing really, to celebrate events. That kind of
ambivalence was infectious and as I became an adult. I also started to mimic that behavior. I even started to get annoyed
when others celebrated their birthdays or bought some giant skeleton down the street.

There wasn't a turning point per say, it was more wanting more from life. I realized that when we celebrate events. That its
the ritual in the tradition that makes them fun and creates really positive memories. You can choose not too but where is the
fun in that. Where is the mark on your soul that says, 'yeah, that was one hell of a year'

So I like putting up holiday lights or giving away candy on Halloween. Its made my life more whole then being a cynic. Thats all.
Thanks for listening.
