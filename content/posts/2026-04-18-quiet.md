## Q is for Quiet

One thing I miss growing up, was visiting my grandparents long gone house. It was in Northern California, pretty far
from the interstate and even far far, at least in memory from much light. I remember summers visiting that were so quiet
and so peaceful with light pollution being at a minimum.

I miss it.
