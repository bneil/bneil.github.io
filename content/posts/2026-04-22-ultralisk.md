## U is for Ultralisk

I don't talk about starcraft. Its one of my favorite games and I absolutely love playing zerg. I don't care how much ive lost.
But there is something about being the swarm. Being the obviously the most adaptable of the species, I loved playing the race.
However, when starcraft 2 kicked off years back, the one thing that really upset me was what they did to my dude. The humble
death elephant known as the Ultralisk.

My man, Jaedong back in the day, who I remember on blue storm used to pull off some amazing traps. I still remember Tastless,
Nick Plott on commenting over the early Kespa leagues. Probably one of my favorites what in the 2006 era. But even Sean Plott
(day 9) always lavishing praise over the humble Ultralisk.

But now. What have we seen in the past few years. My main attack tank, is sad. Barely used. I don't know. Makes me sad people.

_pours one out_

I don't talk much about how much I love starcraft. But god damn, I want my swarm to have its heavy hitter back. Infestors are
pretty neat, but a good zergling w/ ultralisk battering ram is an amazing site. Also, i miss the muta stacks.

Also I don't apologize for posts, but this one was super cathartic, so thank you all for reading.
