## V is for Vertigo

When thinking of a fun word to have with V, I thought it would be fun to admit
a weird weakness I have for elevators. For some reason, every time I leave an elevator
that has ascended after more then a few floors, I get hit with this vertigo that makes
me almost tip over, and leaves my feet feeling gone from earth to moon walking. I don't
know if its an inner ear thing, but I used to dread going to some office buildings because
I would almost fall over every time the elevator stopped.

That's it, confession over.
