## R is for Riddle

So I don't have many original riddles, but when 'R' came around I thought id share it.
So without much ado:

```
If the cyclops from the X-Men, came out one night, after dusk, to the breaking waves of the pacific ocean
and upon looking out, saw a man, not far.. throw a small creature.

The creature flew into the sky, then just on the wind caught the word 'mogwai' on his ear.

Cyclops, saw the creature enveloped by pacific waters. 

A quiet silence. 

Then almost, volcanically the water starts churning and sparking with light.

Only a moment passes, but the sounds of the surf is filled with screams.

Cyclops opens his visor, and focuses, immolating the center of the growing churn of screams. 
```

My question, who prevails? Gismo (Gremlins) or Cyclops?
