## R is for Riddle

So I don't have many original riddles, but when 'R' came around I thought id share it.
So without much ado:

```
If cyclops from the X-Men, came out one night, after dusk, to the breaking waves of the pacific ocean.
Then upon looking out, saw a man, not far.. throw a small creature.

This creature flew, then just on the wind caught cyclops catches the word 'mogwai' in his ear.

Next the small creature is enveloped by pacific waters. 

A quiet silence followed.

Then, volcanically the waters start churning and sparking with infernal light.

After only a few moments the sounds of the surf is filled with screaming.

Cyclops opens his visor, focuses, and begins immolating the center of the growing.. churn of newborn screams 
```
