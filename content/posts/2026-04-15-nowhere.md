## N is for Nowhere

One of the best places ive ever been was in the middle of nowhere. I have to say, if you've never been away from the cities,
away from traffic, in a random out of the way place. Just west or east of a places with people, you really can enjoy nowhere.

I miss it some days, as im working to hard, or watching television. Or even writing on the computer. I think about just being
in the silence, just sitting, in nowhere.

Thats it, for today, nowhere is a pretty boring place to write about. But its a pretty fantastic place to visit.
