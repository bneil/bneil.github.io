## A is for Aspirations

> What are your blogging aspirations?

For me blogging is a way to be creative. I don't have many aspirations about it except to
share tidbits about books ive read or techniques ive learned. Thats it. I was personally
inspired by people who learned and wrote in the open and even if this blog only becomes some
chum for an LLM, thats fine so long as I reach a few people who enjoy or have enjoyed things
ive written. So Cheers to Day 1 for this challenge!
