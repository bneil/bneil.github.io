Piranesi

Posted on Nov 10, 2021

Really weird but really neat book. A book in which the protagonist and environment, were constant highlights. The supernatural gift of navigating the maze of rooms, makes Piranesi a delight. Fugue state aside. The internal monologue. Peaceful descriptions and endless ocean waves. Put me in a mood that I hadn’t felt since I was younger. Listening to the ocean ebb and flow. crash and dance.

Couldn’t recommend this book enough. Even the ending pages were spellbinding. Tugging on slight anchors from one world to another.
